# Armor

**AC subtracts from every hit.** Take the Armor Class off the damage first, then Defend against what remains; anything left lands. A blow of AC or less goes *tink!* — nothing through.

| Kit | AC | *Tink* | Lands |
|---|---|---|---|
| Unarmored / soft | 0 | — | all of it |
| Leather | 1 | 1 | damage −1 |
| Gambeson | 2 | 1–2 | damage −2 |
| Chain | 3 | 1–3 | damage −3 |
| Plate | 4 | 1–4 | damage −4 |
| Full plate / harder | 5+ | 1–5+ | damage −5+ |

Fiction is kit when it wears kit: *that orc is in gambeson*, *mail*, *plate*. Natural hide/shell uses the same words: skeleton **as leather**, crab **as plate**, dragon **as full plate** or harder. Players may ask.

**Armor Breaking:** a new option on a spark:  break armor so that armor provides no AC for the rest of the fight. requires an Armor-piercing weapons (WC>AC). requireds 1 fuel (the normal fuel after the spark) but not required for  **poor quality or ruined armor** (the skeleton's rusty mail, the rotten shield).

**Resistance** (silver, fire…) is AC against its named kind of harm. The referee can use the flat number or roll d6 if the fiction demands swinginess. AC and Resistance stack.

## Quoted Blows

*Referee-facing; the speed rule for monster attacks.*

**Fast referree quotes** Each monster gets a free attack die, but the referee should only roll it when the fiction demands more swinginess. The referee should usually just quote the weapon class as flat damage. This is intentionally player-gameable.

| Weapon | Quote |
|---|---|
| Dagger, arming sword | 2 |
| Falchion, longbow | 3 |
| Halberd / pole arm | 5 |

Natural attacks quote off their damage die when you want the pace: a claw of 1d6 quotes 3.

- **Press with Fuel.** Burn Fuel to add dice **on top of the quote**: a pressed falchion is 3 + 1d6 per die burned.
- **Roll when it matters.** You can always roll the free die instead of quoting, for the swings the fiction cares about.
- **Big monsters roll.** A creature quoted at 2d6 (the crab) can be quoted as the average (3-4 per d6) or rolled. This also works for monsters who have other damage dice, e.g. a zombie is d8 so quote 4.

Twenty orcs loose arrows: twenty quoted 3s against chain AC 3 — *tink*, one sentence, no dice. The two who crept to short range press with a Fuel die each, and those may bite.

## Cracked! and Splatted

Damage through armor comes off HP.

| Monster HP | Result |
|---|---|
| **Above 0** | Still fighting |
| **0 down to −Max HP** | **Cracked!** |
| **Past −Max HP** | **Splatted!** — dead now |

**Cracked!** No longer refills Fuel during Intiative. They may not burn Fuel.

Cracked monsters often whimper off, flee, or surrender. The Ref may check Morale or just have them give it up. The Ref naturally shifts Fuel and attention to whoever is still standing.

Named or solo foes may use full PC Wounds and Shock instead.
