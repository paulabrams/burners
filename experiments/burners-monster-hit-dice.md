# Monster Hit Dice

**Monsters have Hit Dice.** Put **2 × HD** dice on the table — a mix of **white** (armor/shell) and **red** (meat). Poker chips under the mini, or dice-as-tokens. That pile is the life bar. Heat is a separate dish for the whole enemy side.

## Why

Session 3 pain: notepad HP, a shared Heat/HD mishmash, and weak “does it look hurt?” progress. Players dump Fuel and want to see it matter; the Referee wants low bookkeeping and a public track.

**AC 1–2 as a flat soak was a Referee math tax** — players never changed tactics for it. Soft kit does not invent a soak number; it **paints more white into the 2 × HD pile**. Naked / sushi / “kinda tough hide” stay **all red**. Tiny book AC does not invent a soak.

## One rule

1. Player announces **damage** (Fuel faces — “fifteen!”). Keep the big number.
2. Ref: **Hits = ⌈damage ÷ 5⌉** (always round **up**).
3. **Closed?** (white/armor remains, or a flesh weakness you have not paid for) → **−1 Hit**, unless the player **spends 1 Fuel** and claims the **right tool** in fiction — *“I slice the sushi!”* / *“I crack these crabs with my poleax!”* The spent Fuel’s face **adds** to the damage before rounding. Fiction stays in the player’s hands.
4. Apply Hits — **white first, then red**. **0** → bounce.

| Damage | Hits (ceil) | Closed (−1) | Open / right tool |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 1–5 | 1 | 0 | 1 |
| 6–10 | 2 | 1 | 2 |
| 11–15 | 3 | 2 | 3 |
| 16–20 | 4 | 3 | 4 |

| Piece | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| **Hits** | ⌈damage ÷ 5⌉ always. Each Hit plucks one die / pops one card. |
| **Hit Dice** | The pile: **2 × HD** (white + red). |
| **White** | Armor/shell. Peel first. Soft kit **adds whites into the mix**; hard shell → mostly/all white; no kit → all red. |
| **Red** | Meat. Pluck after white is gone (or from the start if sushi). |
| **Right tool** | Player spends **1 Fuel** + names the fiction. Face adds to damage; skip the −1 this blow. Suggest poleax vs shell, slicing vs sushi, silver, holy — but the claim is theirs. |
| **Cracked!** | Last white gone — signal only; no more armor −1 (a flesh weakness still costs Fuel if you claim the right tool). |
| **Heat** | One shared dish — not the Hit Dice pile. Rubric: aim Heat at shown **HD**; lower if disadvantaged, higher if it owns the room. Refresh each round. |
| **Dead** | All dice / cards gone — the monster dies at zero, never below zero. Scoop the stack. For tougher monsters, add levels (hit dice), not negative HP. |

### Paint (mix inside 2 × HD)

| Fiction | Mix |
| --- | --- |
| Naked / sushi / tough hide, no kit | **All red** |
| Soft kit (leather, shield, light gambeson) | **Some white** (orc HD 1 → **1W1R**) |
| Mail / heavier kit | **More white** |
| True shell / plate / scales | **Mostly or all white** (crab HD 3 → **6 white**) |

Do not add a separate armor track outside the 2 × HD budget. Soft armor is “more white,” not “+1 soak forever.”

**Tactics:** big damage while closed; scraps after Cracked. Spend Fuel to claim the right tool when it matters. Players shout damage; the Ref converts.

**Fuel economy:** a **1** and a **2** stacked are 3 → **1 Hit** (ceil); closed → bounce unless right tool. Keep scraps for Defend or to ride a **3+** toward 5+.

## Kit

**Cards (confirmed).** A cheap deck: **face-down = armor** (anonymous turtle), **face-up = meat**. Pre-build a stack per monster (crab: six face-down; Devil sushi: sixteen face-up; orc in leather: one down on one up). As Hits land, **pop cards off onto the table**. When the last face-down is gone, faces show → **Cracked!** Ignore rank and suit. Playing cards are free.

**Remaining dice/chips.** Full **2 × HD** under the mini; pluck as Hits land.

**Inflicted chips only.** Bare minis; add a chip per Hit. Announce the paint or keep the total secret the first time.

- **Heat:** one shared dish (dice). Do not raid it for Hit Dice tokens. Heat ≈ shown HD, nudge for who’s in control.
- Speech: players say **damage** (and optional right-tool claim + Fuel); Ref converts and pops cards.

An older Wåndyr-2 note (*Burners Monster Dice*, vaulted obsolete): **the table shows the fight**; **don’t overload one token**; **pluck the dead**. Leave the POW-tier tables in the vault.

## Multi-foe

Five orcs, an ogre, a warlock, three dire wolves is runnable. Budget is **2 × HD per body**: orcs HD 1 × 5 = **10**; wolves HD 3–4 × 3 ≈ **18–24**; ogre ≈ **8–10**; warlock ≈ **4–6** → ~40–50 chips. Soft kit only changes the **color mix**, not the count.

## Worked example — crabs (HD 3 → 6 white)

Closed while white remains (−1) unless someone spends Fuel to claim poleax/shellbreak.

| Blow | Hits | Pile |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Herrick **15** (no right-tool spend) | ⌈15÷5⌉=3, −1 → **2 white** | **4 white** |
| Reinhardt **10** + spends Fuel (face 4) claiming poleax → dmg **14** | ⌈14÷5⌉=3, no −1 → **3 white** | **1 white** |
| Anyone **6** (no spend) | ⌈6÷5⌉=2, −1 → **1 white** → **Cracked!** | **0** — all-white crab **dead** |

## Worked example — Wet Devil (HD 8 → 16 red)

Sushi — all red. Still **closed** to the wrong edge until someone **spends Fuel** to claim the slice. Otherwise always ceil, then −1.

**Table kit (inflicted):** bare mini; note **16** for yourself; add chips as Hits land. Total public or secret.

Sample dump **5, 6, 11, 5, 10, 9, 2, 3, 2** with no right-tool spends:

| Dmg | Ceil | −1 closed | Chips |
| ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 5 | 1 | 0 | — |
| 6 | 2 | 1 | 1 |
| 11 | 3 | 2 | 3 |
| 5 | 1 | 0 | 3 |
| 10 | 2 | 1 | 4 |
| 9 | 2 | 1 | 5 |
| 2 | 1 | 0 | 5 |
| 3 | 1 | 0 | 5 |
| 2 | 1 | 0 | 5 |

**5 chips** if nobody pays for the slice — she is barely hurt. When Herrick spends Fuel and shouts *“I slice!”*, that blow skips the −1 (and the extra face bumps the total).

## Retired

- Resistance **5** ladder (and Resistance 1–4)
- Flat AC soak (especially AC 1–2 math tax)
- Separate armor-die band *outside* 2 × HD
- Floor/ceil by state (replaced by always-ceil + −1 closed + Fuel right-tool claim)
- Free soft damage while white remains
- White-costs-2 chip pricing
- **Cracked!** = half HP + Spark, then no Heat refresh
- Shared Heat/HD mishmash as the life bar
- Notepad HP as the preferred table track
- Referee-only right tool (weapon auto-opens) — players claim it with Fuel + fiction
