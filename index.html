Burners

A little good, a little bad, a little of both.

Fast, lethal sword-and-sorcerie in the Dragon Isles. Fire makes it good.

What is a Burner?

Respectable folk spit the word. Burners — the drifters who blow into a harbor town on the evening tide with a sword, a spell, or a set of picks, take the job nobody sane will touch, and are gone by the next fog. You are not here to save the Dragon Isles; you are here to get in, get paid, and get out with your skin — and if the place is on fire behind you as you go, well… it usually is.
