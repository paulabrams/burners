# Cracked!

*Proposal — not canonical yet.*

While whole, a monster has a **free defense die** and a **free attack die** (no Fuel). On each blow, roll **two dice**:

1. **Die 1 + AC** = defense (AC 0 if unarmored; Fuel optional for shield/cover).
2. **Die 1 is a 6 (Spark)** → spend Die 2 now as **more defense** (add it; chain on further 6s) or a **riposte** (counter/grab; defense stays 6+AC). Defensive foes (crab, plate) usually add defense; ferocious foes (zombie, boar) usually riposte.
3. Else: optionally add Die 2 vs a weak hit, or keep it as the free attack if **this** monster goes next (else unused).
4. Blow ≤ defense → *ting!* Still whole. Blow > defense → **Cracked!** Leftover (`blow − defense`) to HP; narrate what broke (shell, mail, wing, swarm cohesion…).

**After Cracked:** no free dice — Fuel only; further blows hit HP raw. Dead at 0 HP as usual.

Named Resistance (silver, fire…) stays separate. PC AC unchanged.
