# Burners Muster

*Ok boys, check your kit, check your weapons, it's today. You get a pint and a chaser, no more. Let's go*

**Boss**, **retainer**, **Muster**, **Muster slots**. Framed after OSE [Retainers](https://oldschoolessentials.necroticgnome.com/srd/index.php/Retainers). Heart 1+ unlocks Muster in [[Burners Adventure Game]].

Someone left at camp or castle is not on the adventure. Week-rate services and garrison wages live on [[Burners Equipment]]; they are not this page until they march with you. A specialist (or anyone else) who ends up on the job — a rescued prisoner, a sage dragged into the barrow — is a **retainer for that stretch**: same sheet, Loyalty, XP, and Muster rules.

---

## Retainers

NPCs hired to share the risks of adventuring with the party. They will not keep working if treated poorly; a disgruntled retainer spreads word and makes hiring harder.

Anyone with coin can hire. Only a **Boss (Heart 1+)** **Musters** — splitting the twelve into physical kit and **Muster slots**. How many Muster slots you declare is the Burners answer to OSE’s Charisma retainer limit.

**Recruitment.** Taverns, help-wanted notices, dockside crews, debts called in. The Referee plays the applicants. Explain the job and the share; Reaction roll (see [[Burners Referee Guide]]); Heart on the roll if you have it. A fair outfit and a clear half-share ease it; bad reputation hardens it.

**Pay.** No up-front wage for the adventure. You **equip** them from your purse (arms, kit, food on the road). Survivors of the job take at least a **half-share** of treasure taken — that is their pay. Flat coin alone will not buy a delve.

**Sheet.** Half-power track (below). Usually starts **level 1**, climbs on XP, never above the hiring Boss (or lead PC). **Sword or Craft only** — never Sorcerie, never Heart, never Muster. By default they **put themselves first**. **Loyalty 7.**

**XP.** Share the pot evenly with everyone present on the job. A **Mustering** retainer keeps a **full** share — the Boss plays them like a PC. A retainer **not** Mustering keeps **half** of their award (OSE: they follow orders). Levels in civilization on the Burner XP table. Full procedure: [[Burners Experience]].

**Loyal** is a **tag** after a real bond (Referee's call). +3 Loyalty; when they hold, they **act loyally** — prioritize their patron, Block for them, stay on their heel. Abuse the bond and the tag can fall — word travels.

**Starter (Boss).** A starting Boss begins with one **loyal retainer** at level 1: Sword 1 or Craft 1, 2 Traits, HP = **1d3**, Loyalty **10** (7+3), basic kit paid from the Boss's purse, and a **half-share** of the take when they live.

## Half Power

Retainers are not Burners. **Everything that scales with level or Approach is halved, round down** (see *Manage* for the only round-up):

| Piece | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| **Traits** | **2 per retainer level** (half of a Burner's 4) |
| **Signatures** | At most **⌊Approach / 2⌋** per Approach — earned in play, never bought |
| **HP** | **1d3 per retainer level** (about 2 per level on average; minimum **1**). On level-up: reroll the whole total (keep higher of new total or old+1). |
| **Fuel level dice / refill** | **⌊retainer level / 2⌋**, minimum **1** |
| **Veteran extras** | **⌊Sword / 2⌋** extras (Sword 1 → one attack) |
| **Approach rolls** | **`2d6 + ⌊Approach / 2⌋`** |
| **Shock and other Approach math** | Same half (Shock: **`2d6 + ⌊Sword / 2⌋`**) |

**Not halved:** shares, Muster slots (**1 per retainer**), Loyalty, kit on their person. Armor still soaks. Full Wound and Shock once HP is spent.

### Who plays them

- **Mustered:** like a second sheet — the **Boss's player** plays and rolls. No fight Loyalty checks while the Boss is **active**.
- **Not Mustering** (or hired by a non-Boss): **NPCs** run by the Referee; Loyalty checks fire more often (see *Loyalty*).

After-adventure Loyalty (will they stay?) still belongs to the Referee for everyone.

### Manage (Boss Action)

**Default:** half, always round **down**.

**Managed:** The Boss spends **their Action** that round to **manage** the Muster — the Boss takes no other Action that round. Until the next Roll Initiative, up to **Heart** Mustering retainers (your choice if you Muster more than Heart) use **half round up** for Approach rolls, Shock, Veteran extras, and Fuel level-die counts. HP on the sheet does not rewrite mid-fight. Excess Mustering retainers stay at half down.

- One Action → up to **Heart** Mustering retainers that round.
- Only retainers you are Mustering.
- Managing again next round costs another Action (no Boss Action of your own that round).

## Traits

Job names are Traits, tagged to Sword or Craft. A retainer fills their item slots for that work; the Boss outfits upgrades from the purse.

**Packbearers** carry baggage and keep simple items handy: rope, waterskin, and the like.

**Fighting men** hold Sword-tagged Traits for preferred arms and kit — spear, arming sword, shield.

**Lantern bearers** (linkboys, torchbearers) hold *lantern-bearer* or *torchbearer*. Lantern or torches, flint and steel, oil.

**Shield bearers** hold *shield* / shield-bearer. Chief work: resupplying fighters with fresh boards.

**Squires** keep weapons ready (*"Squire, my sword!"*). They may help a fighter don armor.

---

## Animals (not Muster)

Muster seats **people**. A mule bought to haul, or a horse you ride, is a **bought asset** — off the twelve, not a Muster slot. Prices: [[Burners Equipment#Animals and mounts]].

A **trained, named** companion (your warhorse, your dog) is a **Signature** on the Trait that covers the work — not a retainer and not a Muster slot. See *Signatures* on [[Burners Adventure Game]].

## Muster Slots

Each Mustering retainer costs **one Muster slot**. One person, one slot. Care and treatment modify **Loyalty** — not slot cost.

### Muster (Heart 1+)

Before a job — in town or at the safehouse — or on any **8-hour sleep**, the Boss **Musters**:

1. Declare the **physical / Muster** split of the twelve.
2. Place named retainers — **one Muster slot each**.
3. Lock the Muster for the run until the next Muster.

Going alone: **0** Muster slots, all twelve physical. Retainers wait at home — off this page until Mustering.

Muster slots hold **people**, not gear. Kit rides on them; outfit and new gear come from the Boss's purse. On Roll Initiative the Boss adds **one die per Muster slot declared** (filled or empty). Those slots never also count as empty-inventory dice. See [[Burners Adventure Game]].

Muster slots buy packing, Fuel, Initiative, Manage, **Boss-player control**, **no fight Loyalty while the Boss is active**, and **Heart on Loyalty checks** — not a different XP track.

**Non-Bosses** may still hire retainers. Without Muster they do not gain Muster-slot Initiative; the Referee runs those NPCs.

## Loyalty

Retainers use **Loyalty**, not monster Morale. When a check is called, the Referee rolls **2d6**: if the roll is **≤ Loyalty** (with modifiers), they **hold**; if higher, they **break** — flee, freeze, drop the torch, demand to leave, or quit after the job. Play it by fiction.

**Base Loyalty 7.** **Loyal** tag: **+3**.

**Mustered.** Played by the Boss's player, as a PC would be. **No Loyalty checks in a fight** while the Boss is **active**. If the Boss is down, fled, captured, or otherwise out of the fight, Loyalty checks resume — including extreme peril. After the adventure, check whether they stay on (as OSE). Whenever a Mustering retainer checks Loyalty, **add the Boss's Heart** — unless the Boss is down, fled, or absent (then Heart does not apply).

**Not Mustering.** Referee NPCs. **Check more often** in the press — ordinary fight scares, first ally felled, side half hurt or dropping, clearly losing, or any shock the Referee judges — plus after the job. No automatic Heart.

**Common modifiers** (Referee's call, as in OSE):

| Situation | Typical mod |
| --- | --- |
| Mustering retainer (Boss active) | + Boss's **Heart** (always) |
| Well kept (outfitted, fed, fair half-share, decent treatment) | +1 |
| Neglected (shorted share, cold, mistreated, sent unarmed) | −1 or −2 |
| Boss down, fled, or absent | −2 (no Heart) |
| Overwhelming foe or sudden disaster | −1 or −2 |
| Generous treasure promised or just paid | +1 |

A **loyal** retainer who holds **acts loyally** — they prioritize their patron over their own skin when the fiction allows. An ordinary retainer who holds still looks to their own hide first.

Without a Boss, the Referee checks against base Loyalty with situational mods only — and uses the **not Mustering** cadence.

## Garrison (pointer)

Month-hire soldiers for walls and armies are OSE [mercenaries](https://oldschoolessentials.necroticgnome.com/srd/index.php/Mercenaries) — not adventure companions. Rates and notes: [[Burners Equipment#Hirelings and specialists]]. If individuals march into a delve with the PCs, treat them as retainers for that stretch.

## Becoming a Burner

A retainer becomes a full **Burner** only when they become a **PC**. Until then they stay on the half-power track.

On that claim: PC **at their current retainer level**. Rebuild under full PC rules — 4 Traits per level, full HP, full Approach math, one Signature per Approach level, open Approaches.

---

## Professional Adventurers

The Referee may field a rare **professional adventurer**: an NPC built as a PC. Uncommon. Not a retainer upgrade path.

## Example Retainers

All examples use half power (round down) unless a Boss **manages** them that round (up to Heart at once). Each Mustering retainer costs **1** Muster slot.

### Cob — lantern bearer

- **Level 1, Craft 1.** HP **1d3**. Loyalty **7**.
- **Traits (2):** *lantern-bearer*, *quiet* (Craft).
- **Kit:** hooded lantern, flask of oil, flint and steel, spare torch.
- Half-share when the haul comes home.

### Merrin — packbearer

- **Level 1, Craft 1.** HP **1d3**. Loyalty **7**.
- **Traits (2):** *packbearer*, *broad-backed* (Craft).
- **Kit:** rope, waterskins, spare rations, room for the haul.
- Road and barrow; climbs on XP.

### Bren — shield bearer

- **Level 1, Sword 1.** HP **1d3**. Loyalty **7** (+1 well kept / −1 neglected).
- **Traits (2):** *shield-bearer*, *steady* (Sword).
- **Kit:** two heaters, spare buckler, leather.
- Puts self first.

### Tam — squire

- **Level 1, Sword 1.** HP **1d3**. Loyalty **7**.
- **Traits (2):** *squire*, *arming sword* (Sword).
- **Kit:** master's spare blade, oil, strap kit.
- Proud of the harness; looks to his own skin when the line bucks.

### Nell — spear (loyal)

- **Level 1, Sword 1.** HP **1d3**. Loyalty **10** (7+3 loyal).
- **Traits (2):** *spear*, *steady under fire* (Sword).
- **Kit:** spear, gambeson (AC 2), kettle helm.
- Starter bond for a green Boss. Outfitted from the Boss's purse; half-share when she lives. When she holds, she prioritizes the Boss.
